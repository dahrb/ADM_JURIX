About Argumentation Decision Frameworks (ADMs)

Argumentation Decision Frameworks (ADMs) are formal structures that enable systematic decision-making through logical argumentation. They provide a structured approach to evaluating complex scenarios by breaking down decisions into logical components and relationships.

Structured decision analysis through logical argumentation
Support for complex dependencies and sub-decision models
Visual representation of decision networks
Algorithmic processing of decision criteria
